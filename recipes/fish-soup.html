Fish Soup

Descrition

Baja fish soup is the simplest, nutritious dish that can be prepared by the fisherman in half an hour (you only need to add salt beforehand), the density of which is naturally determined by the amount of fish and especially water used, as well as 2-3 heads of onion.

Ingredients

  • 3 kg fish
  • 3 big onions
  • 3 tablespoon paprika
  • salt
  • water
  • match dough

Steps

  1. Clean the fish and set aside the fish sauce and milk. Grind, slice, then add salt to taste.
  2. Set aside for 1 hour.
  3. Clean the red onions and cut them into small cubes.
  4. At the end of the hour, put the fish slices and the onion in the pot first, then pour 3.6 l of water.
  5. Boil the juice. When the foam has boiled off, sprinkle the paprika.
  6. After 15-20 minutes, add the giblets and the hot cherry pepper, then you need another 20 minutes of cooking. It will be good if the paprika does not stick out on the edge of the spoon when tasting.
  7. Meanwhile, prepare the matcha dough in a separate pot.
